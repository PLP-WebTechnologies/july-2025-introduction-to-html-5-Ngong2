What is Semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML uses tags that convey the meaning of the content they contain. It improves accessibility and helps search engines understand the structure of your site.

Accessibility Best Practices

Accessibility ensures that all users, including those with disabilities, can navigate and understand your website. Use semantic landmarks such as <header>, <nav>, <main>, and <footer> to define the structure. Include descriptive headings, provide alt text for all images, and use ARIA attributes like aria-label or aria-labelledbyto enhance support for screen readers.

SEO Fundamentals

Use heading tags in the correct order, include relevant keywords in content and metadata, and keep your HTML well-structured. Descriptive titles and meta descriptions help search engines understand your page.
